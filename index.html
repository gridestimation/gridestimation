Our Construction Estimating Services

Reliable Estimating Partner Trusted Across Canada

Grid Estimations is a reliable construction estimating partner trusted by contractors and builders in Canada. We have been in the construction industry for 16 years, providing accurate construction cost estimates and detailed construction takeoff services to contractors and construction managers. Our estimates have been used to bid jobs, order materials, and plan construction schedules.

We calculate costs for every type of construction project, from 1,000 sq ft starter homes to 500,000 sq ft industrial facilities, by using advanced construction estimating software to measure quantities from your drawings. Our pricing is also market competitive. We charge project to project, not a fixed amount or percentage.

Comprehensive Estimating Solutions

Cost Estimating Services

Whether it's a residential home or a large commercial build, or an industrial project, we break down every cost element including materials, labour, equipment, and overhead, to give you a clear picture of your project budget following MasterFormat standards.

Construction Takeoff Services

We provide accurate construction takeoff services that help contractors order materials, schedule deliveries, and allocate labour crews, backed by expert estimators with 12-20 years of field construction experience.

Residential Estimating Services

Home builders and residential contractors use our residential estimating services to bid custom builds, spec developments, and renovation projects, accounting for Canadian residential building codes.

Commercial Construction Estimating

Handling projects from 5,000 sq ft retail spaces to 300,000 sq ft office towers with expertise in Canadian commercial codes, LEED certification, and accessibility standards.

Preliminary Estimating Services

Also called conceptual estimates, we use cost-per-square-foot models and regional cost indices to provide solid budget parameters for early-stage planning and land purchase decisions.

Industrial Estimating Services

Handling projects ranging from $2 million equipment installations to $200 million processing facilities, providing detailed breakdowns and precise material estimates.
